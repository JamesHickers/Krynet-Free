Krynet Terms & Security Policies

Terms of Service

By using Krynet, you agree not to misuse the platform for illegal activities. Krynet is for secure, anonymous communication only. Abuse may result in IP blacklisting.

Privacy Policy

Krynet does not collect personal data, IPs, or device fingerprints. No logs are saved. We use local client-side encryption and store nothing on our servers.

Encryption Details

All messages use AES-256 and RSA-2048 encryption. End-to-end by design, with no backdoors. Once messages are read, they are unrecoverable.
